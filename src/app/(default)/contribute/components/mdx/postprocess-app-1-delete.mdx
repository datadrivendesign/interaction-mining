## Post-Process on Mobile App
Post-processing actions can be done on the mobile app before upload.

On the app you can:
- Delete extraneous or irrelevant screens from a task flow.
- Tag missing or incorrect gestures and interactions.
- Redact sensitive information from screens based on view hierarchy boxes.

---

### Delete Screens

To delete unneeded screens from your task flow:

1. Review your trace and locate extraneous screen(s).
2. Long press target screen(s) to open up a menu bar on the upper right side.
3. Tap the trash icon (right side) to delete screen(s).
